Practical guides

Before you go

Hard-won lessons distilled into actionable tips. Skip the mistakes, keep the adventures.

01

Pack half, travel twice as far

Everything fits in a 30L backpack. You'll thank yourself on every bus, train and mountain path.

02

Book the first night only

Arrive knowing where you'll sleep. After that, let conversations and weather decide the plan.

03

Learn 10 words per country

Hello, thank you, please, how much, and delicious. Doors open that otherwise stay shut.

04

Slow down at 70% speed

Cut your itinerary by 30%. The empty hours are where the real travel actually happens.
